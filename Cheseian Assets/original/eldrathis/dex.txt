Eldrathis
(Legendary) - The Mystic Sentinel
Type(s) Psychic/Fairy
This Pokemon is said to be the source of lost magical knowledge, whispering forgotten spells to those who seek it. It was once revered in Dawngrove, but when its teachings led to reckless power, it vanished into a hidden dimension.
Has the appearance of Bison with runes covering it's body. It shifts between being physical and ethereal.
