Draklash
Type(s): Dragon/Dark
Catagory: "Punishment Pokémon"
Dex 1: Draklash is a hulking, fearsome Pokemon bringing destruction to those who commit evil, and leaving nothing but fear in its wake. They wander the regions mountains and ruins.
Dex 2: Draklash feeds on the souls of the wicked, growing stronger with each soul it claims. It is said that the Empress/Ruler of the Region has a mysterious alliance with the Pokémon.
